Privacy Policy for MSNAudioBookRead

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

MSNAudioBookRead is a personal-use Android application designed to play mp3 audiobook files located in the /Music/BOOKS directory on your device. This app is not intended for general public use or for children.

Information Collection

MSNAudioBookRead does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app accesses the device's MediaStore to locate and play audio files. It also interfaces with Android Auto to allow audio playback while driving.

For user convenience, the app stores playback progress locally on your device. This includes:

  • Last played file name
  • Folder location within /Music/BOOKS
  • Last playback position (frame or timestamp)

All this data remains on your device and is not shared with anyone or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or external data processors.

Permissions

The app requests permission to access your device's storage through the MediaStore API. This is solely to locate and play mp3 files in the /Music/BOOKS directory. It does not access or process any other files or data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children. It is designed exclusively for the personal use of the developer and is not distributed for use by others.

Data Security

All data related to playback progress is stored locally on the device. No information is transmitted off the device or to any external services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is unlikely to change since the app is intended solely for personal use. However, if any changes occur, this page will be updated accordingly.
